What Is Virtual DJ?
Virtual DJ is a free program for mixing, connecting and applying effects to audio files with instant playback.
Name: Virtual DJ
Version: v2018 (Latest)
Size: 32.2 MB


Main Features of Virtual DJ

The special convenience of working in this program makes it possible to accelerate and slow down the tempo in the received music, both manually and automatically. You can synchronize songs by speed, tempo and much more.
1. Memorizes up to 9 positions in the track.
2. Simulates vinyl records.
3. Automatically "adjust" the speed.
4. It has a 3-band equalizer.
5. There are various types of plug-ins for sound processing.
6. Compatible with 32 and 64 bit Windows 7, 8 and 10.
